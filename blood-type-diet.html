For quick reference only. The Blood Type Diet was proposed by Dr. Peter J. D'Adamo in Eat Right 4 Your Type. Current scientific evidence does not support tailoring diet to blood type. This page summarises his recommendations and is not medical advice — talk to a doctor or dietitian before making changes.
